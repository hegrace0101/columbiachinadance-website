About Us

Founded in 2013, Columbia China Dance is the only dance group at Columbia University that focuses on traditional Chinese dance from around the country.

Our Mission:

  • Promote Chinese Traditional Dance to the Columbia University community and New York City through various performances
  • Give dancers the opportunity to learn a diverse range of Chinese ethnic styles through practices and rehearsals
  • Share the team's hardwork and artistry in an annual showcase hosted by Columbia China Dance

Fall 2020 Programming

The board has taken the summer to carefully restructure Columbia China Dance's programming in the fall to accommodate for online instruction through Zoom. The changes are outlined below:

  1. There will be no auditions this semester and all are welcome to attend rehearsals and events offered by the club

Board

Meet the board and our amazing coach! Hover over or click on each member to learn more about them.

I’m Qianni Yu, I come from China and am a graduate student at Columbia School of Social Work. I've learned Chinese dance for over 20 years. I have won the 1st Prize of National College Art Festival and also got the 1st Prize of Dance Competition at Zhejiang Province in China. I really love Chinese dance. For me, it is not only traditional Chinese art but also a way for people from all around the world to know more about Chinese culture and history. I’m very honored to be the president of the China Dance team. I hope with our efforts, we can bring our audiences more enjoyable dance shows in the future.

Qianni Yu

President

Through Chinese dance I have been able to further embrace and learn about my cultural heritage as well as spread the beauty of Asian art within the community. Outside of school work, I spend my time binging the latest show that I have discovered, carefully planning my return to campus, and baking to satisfy my quarantine cravings.
